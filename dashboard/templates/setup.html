Welcome

Quantyra PaaS is a self-hosted infrastructure management platform that helps you:

  • Deploy and manage applications across multiple servers
  • Provision domains with automatic SSL certificates
  • Manage databases in a PostgreSQL cluster
  • Monitor infrastructure with Prometheus and Grafana

Your configuration will be stored in a local SQLite database at /data/paas.db. Secrets are encrypted with AES-256-GCM.
